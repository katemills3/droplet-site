Getting there


History Colorado is located at 12th and Broadway, just three blocks south of the capitol building. Uber or Lyft are probably the easiest modes of transportation and can be taken from anywhere in the city or suburbs.

If you drive, parking is directly north of the museum at the 1290 Broadway garage. It costs $7 to park. There's also metered street parking nearby. Free street parking can be found after 3 pm if you venture a bit further into the Golden Triangle neighborhood (west of Broadway and south of 12th).
